Cheese Burger and Rice Bowl

"More protein, less calories than a Big Mac and waaaay better for you."

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 150g 90/10 ground beef
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Low fat cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato

    • Sauce (optional)

  • 1.5 tbsp light mayo
  • 1tsp ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp mustard
  • 1/2 tsp relish
  • Paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. In a pan cook 150g 90/10 ground beef, add onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  2. Dice lettuce, onion, and tomato.
  3. (Optional) Combine sauce ingredients.
  4. Assemble bowl with 150g white rice, cooked beef, 30g low fat cheese, pickles, lettuce, tomato, onions and sauce.
