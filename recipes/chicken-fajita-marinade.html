Chicken Fajitas Marinade

Marinade for chicken fajitas from the Food Charlatan

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs chicken (thighs or breast)
  • 2 tbsp lime juicee
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp cumin
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Olive or vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. (Optional) Slice, against the grain, 1 1/2 pounds chicken into long 1-inch wide strips.
  2. Add all the marinade ingredients to a bowl: 2 tablespoons lime juice, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon liquid smoke, 2 tablespoons cumin, 2 teaspoons kosher salt, 2 teaspoons chili powder, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/3 cup chopped cilantro, and 4 cloves of garlic (at least 1 tablespoon minced). Stir until well mixed. Add olive oil to increase volume as desired (more if you would like to use left over marinade for a sautee).

    Add chicken into bowl and make sure it's completely covered.
